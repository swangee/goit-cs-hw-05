
Beyond the known world, in the abyssal void, an ancient force stirred. The Void, a realm of insatiable hunger, sent its heralds to consume all life in Runeterra. Kai’Sa, a survivor who had bonded with the Void to stay alive, walked a fine line between humanity and monstrosity. Her presence was both a warning and a shield against the horrors that sought to consume her world.

Kha’Zix, a predator from the Void, evolved with each kill, growing ever more dangerous. Meanwhile, Malzahar, the Prophet of the Void, spread its influence across Shurima, sowing the seeds of an apocalypse. As the Void’s tendrils reached farther, unlikely alliances formed among Runeterra’s champions, united by a single goal: survival.
