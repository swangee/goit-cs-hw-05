
In the heart of Demacia, where justice and honor reign supreme, a young mage named Sylas began to question the very foundations of his homeland. Born with the rare ability to sense magic, Sylas was taken from his family to serve the kingdom as a mage-seeker. Yet, as he grew older, he realized the hypocrisy of Demacia’s ideals. Those in power wielded magic in secret while condemning others for the same.

Sylas’s rebellion against Demacia ignited a civil war. Lux, torn between her loyalty to her brother Garen and her empathy for Sylas, faced impossible choices. The kingdom teetered on the brink, its shining image tarnished by shadows of betrayal. Would justice prevail, or would Demacia fall to the chaos it sought to suppress?
