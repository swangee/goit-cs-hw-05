
In the twin cities of Piltover and Zaun, innovation and chaos walked hand in hand. Caitlyn, the Sheriff of Piltover, worked tirelessly to maintain order in a city driven by technological progress. Below the gleaming streets, in the smog-filled underbelly of Zaun, Jinx, a manic criminal, wreaked havoc with her explosive antics.

Vi, a former Zaunite enforcer turned Piltover’s peacekeeper, grappled with her own past as Jinx’s estranged sister. As tensions between the two cities escalated, a new threat emerged: a rogue inventor whose unchecked experiments threatened to tear both societies apart. The balance between progress and destruction hung by a thread, and the choices of a few could shape the future of both cities.
